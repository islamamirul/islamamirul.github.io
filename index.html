Amirul Islam

Toronto, May 2020

I am a senior researcher at Huawei Noah's Ark Lab, Toronto. My primary research interests are multi-modal learning, Generative AI, and human-centric AI (e.g., explainability, interpretability, generalization, fairness and bias). I received my Ph.D. in Computer Science from Ryerson University in 2022, advised by Neil Bruce and Kosta Derpanis. Previously, I received my M.Sc. in Computer Science from the University of Manitoba, where I was advised by Yang Wang and Neil Bruce. I am a recipient of the Vector Institute Postgraduate Affilite.
